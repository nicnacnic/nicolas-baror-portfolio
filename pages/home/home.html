About Nicolas

Nicolas is a current undergraduate student studying Theatre Design and Technology at Penn State University. His love for theatre stems back to his middle school days, where he was a costumer on a production of Fiddler on the Roof Jr. Since then, Nicolas has worked on countless amazing productions for many different companies in his time as a lighting designer and technical director.

Nicolas' philosophy is simple: through detailed planning and effective communication, make any project the best it can be within the constraints. If you'd like to see what Nicolas can do for your upcoming project, make sure to contact him today!
