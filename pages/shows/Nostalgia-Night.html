Nostalgia Night

Commissioned by the Penn State New Musicals Initiative, Nostalgia Night follows Laughlin High's Class of 2021 during their graduation party at Envideon, a underage dance club set in the 1980's. Wanna dance? Photobooth? Get hypnotized? Take things too far? Do that thing you always wanted to do, say that thing you always wanted to say, before it’s too late? Laughlin High School Class of 2021 – who do you wanna be?

Role: Light Board Operator, Electrician, Carpenter
