Indiethon

Indiethon is a tri-annual online charity event with a focus on showing speedruns of indie games. What's a speedrun? Well, it's when a person, through practice, tries to beat a video game as fast as possible by showing off tricks and shortcuts that aren't otherwise done in normal gameplay.

As the main organizer, I oversee all aspects of Indiethon's operations. This includes planning for the next event, staff management, developing our streaming technology, and much more. Everything that we do is unpaid and in our free time, our organization exists because of my staff's commitment to doing something good and raising money for charity.

To date, we have raised over $9,000 for our various charity partners, and we see no reason to stop now! We have focused strictly on online events, however, we are hoping to jump to in-person events as soon as we have the staff and capital to support it.
